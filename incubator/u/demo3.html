Lecture Description

We also have a Knowledge Base article on creating a live session. Click here  to view.


In this video lesson, I go over a few things:

00:00   Learning Objectives
  • Understand how to set up a Live Session
  • Know how to use Live Session as a supplement to your course
00:03   Schedule a Live Session
00:22   Live Session Notification

You and your students can see any scheduled live session on your course's dashboard. Your students will also be notified by email once you schedule a live session.

00:29   How to Join a Live Session
1 hour prior to the start of your live session, your students will be able to join a "lobby" to wait until you start the session; they will not have access to Udemy Live. You, as the instructor, will be able to enter Udemy Live and prepare for your session.
00:35   Initial Set Up - Select a Layout
As an instructor, you need to select the default view or display for your live session. 
00:53   Explanation of Tools in Live Session
  • Participation List
  • File Sharing
  • Audio and Video Sharing
  • Presentation Sharing
  • Chat Window
01:16   Udemy Live Settings
01:34   How to Use the Live Session Tool in Your Course
Hold real-time office hours, workshops, or 1-on-1 consultations.
